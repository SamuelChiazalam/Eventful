## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
A: Yes! All payments are processed through Paystack with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies are set by event creators. Contact them directly for refund requests.

**Q: How do I contact an event creator?**
A: Creator contact information is available on the event details page.

### For Creators

**Q: When do I receive payment?**
A: Payments are instantly verified. Settlement to your bank account follows Paystack's payout schedule (typically 2-3 business days).

**Q: Can I edit an event after publishing?**
A: Yes! You can edit event details anytime. Attendees are notified of major changes.

**Q: What happens if I cancel an event?**
A: All ticket holders receive automatic notifications. Handle refunds through your Paystack dashboard.

**Q: Is there a fee for using Eventful?**
A: Eventful platform is free! Paystack charges standard payment processing fees (1.5% + â‚¦100 per transaction).

### For Eventees

**Q: What if I lose my ticket?**
A: Check your email or login to "My Tickets" dashboard to retrieve it anytime.

**Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?**
A: Currently tickets are non-transferable (tied to purchaser's account).

**Q: What if the QR code doesn't work?**
A: Use your unique ticket number as backup. Event staff can verify manually.

**Q: Can I buy multiple tickets?**
A: Currently one ticket per transaction. Make multiple purchases for multiple tickets.
